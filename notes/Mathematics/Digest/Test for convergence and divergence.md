## Method1
#### *integral test*
$\text{for }0 < a_{n+1} < a_{n}$ 
$\text{if }\int ^{\infty} a_{n}\text{ converges, then }\sum ^{\infty} a_{n}\text{ also converges}$

#### *Ratio test*
$\text{ for }0<a_{n+1}<a_{n}$
$\text{ if }\lim_{ n \to \infty }\frac{a_{n+1}}{a_{n}} =0$$\text{ then } \sum ^{\infty}a_{n}\text{ also converges }$
